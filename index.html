About RRDCH

Established in 1992, Rajarajeshwari Dental College and Hospital has grown into a premier institution of dental education and research in India. Affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS) and recognized by the Dental Council of India (DCI).

Our mission is to foster comprehensive dental care, innovative research, and produce distinguished dental professionals.

  • ✓ Recognized by Dental Council of India (DCI)
  • ✓ Affiliated to RGUHS, Karnataka
  • ✓ ISO 9001:2015 Certified Institution
