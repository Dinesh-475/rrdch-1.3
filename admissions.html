Admission Process

Step 1 — NEET Qualification

Appear for NEET-UG (BDS) or NEET-MDS (MDS) conducted by NTA / NBE.

Step 2 — Counselling by RGUHS / KEA

Attend state/central counselling. RRDCH participates via RGUHS round for Karnataka students.

Step 3 — Document Verification

Produce 10th, 12th, NEET scorecard, community certificate, and photographs at RRDCH admissions office.

Step 4 — Fee Payment & Enrollment

Complete fee payment, hostel booking, and receive your college ID and timetable.
