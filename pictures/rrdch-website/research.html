🔬 Research Facilities

Real-Time PCR Lab

Advanced molecular diagnostics facility attracting research collaborations from colleges across India. Used for oral cancer biomarker studies.

CBCT Imaging Unit (2015)

Cone Beam Computed Tomography for 3D pathological and structural analysis. Used in research on TMJ disorders and implant planning.

Histopathology Lab

Full histopathology and cytology laboratory with fluorescence microscopes and cryostat for biopsy and oral cancer research.
