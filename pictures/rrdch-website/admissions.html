📋 BDS Eligibility & Process

Eligibility Criteria:

  • ✓10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology (PCB)
  • ✓Minimum 50% marks (General) / 40% (SC/OBC)
  • ✓Valid NEET-UG score
  • ✓Minimum age: 17 years as on Dec 31 of admission year
  • ✓Indian citizens (NRI quota available)

Admission Process:

1
NEET-UG Qualification
Score valid NEET-UG marks in the current academic year.
2
State/KEA Counselling
Participate in Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) or State Counselling for seat allotment.
3
Document Verification
Report to RRDCH with original documents for verification within specified date.
4
Fee Payment & Admission
Pay fees and complete admission formalities. Receive student ID and login credentials.
