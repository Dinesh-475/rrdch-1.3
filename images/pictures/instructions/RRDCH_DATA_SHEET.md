## 🏥 DEPARTMENTS (ALL 10 — REAL)

1. **Oral Medicine & Radiology** — CBCT, X-rays, TMJ disorders, oral medicine diagnosis
2. **Prosthodontics, Crown & Bridge** — Dentures, crowns, bridges, full mouth rehabilitation
3. **Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery** — Extractions, jaw surgery, orthognathic, implants, trauma
4. **Periodontology** — Gum disease, scaling, flap surgeries, bone grafting
5. **Pedodontics & Preventive Dentistry** — Children's dentistry, fluoride, sealants
6. **Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics** — Root canals, fillings, restoration, rotary endo
7. **Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics** — Braces, aligners, jaw alignment, pediatric ortho
8. **Public Health Dentistry** — Community camps, awareness programs, preventive dentistry
9. **Oral Pathology & Microbiology** — Biopsies, oral cancer screening, pathological diagnosis
10. **Oral Implantology** (Certificate) — Implant placement, bone augmentation, guided surgery

   - Variable name: `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY`
   - Model to use: `claude-sonnet-4-20250514`
   - Get at: https://console.anthropic.com

2. **Google Maps Embed API Key** (optional — embed works without key for basic maps)
   - Variable name: `GOOGLE_MAPS_API_KEY`
   - Get at: https://console.cloud.google.com

3. **Email SMTP Credentials** — for appointment confirmation emails (Nodemailer)
   - Variables: `EMAIL_USER`, `EMAIL_PASS`
   - Recommended: Gmail with App Password
