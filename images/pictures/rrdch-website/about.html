Who We Are

A Legacy of Excellence in Dental Education

Rajarajeswari Dental College and Hospital (RRDCH) is an NAAC-accredited institution (Cycle 2 Grade A; refer to official disclosures for Cycle 3), established in 1992 under the Moogambigai Charitable and Educational Trust, led by Chairman Dr. A.C. Shanmugam.

Affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS), Bangalore, and recognized by the Dental Council of India (DCI), RRDCH stands as one of India's premier dental institutions.

Since 2014, RRDCH serves as the Royal College of Physicians & Surgeons of Glasgow, UK — MFDS Parts 1 & 2 Examination Centre, the only such centre in South Karnataka.

Our Journey

History Timeline

32 years of growth, achievements, and milestones that shaped RRDCH.

1992
Foundation of RRDCH

Rajarajeswari Dental College and Hospital established by the Moogambigai Charitable and Educational Trust under the leadership of Dr. A.C. Shanmugam. First batch of 40 BDS students enrolled.

1997
MDS Programs Launched

Postgraduate MDS programs introduced across 5 specializations, with affiliation from RGUHS Bangalore. Faculty strength expanded to 60+.

2003
New Hospital Building — 250 Dental Units

State-of-the-art 2 lakh sq. ft. building inaugurated with 250 electronic dental units across 10 specialized clinics. Daily patient capacity expands to accommodate 500 patients.

2008
ISO 9001:2008 Certification

Received ISO 9001:2008 quality management certification from BSICS, signifying international quality standards in education and patient care.

2012
NAAC 'A' Grade Accreditation

Awarded coveted NAAC 'A' Grade accreditation — recognizing outstanding academic quality, research output, and infrastructure excellence.

2014
Glasgow Royal College MFDS Centre

RRDCH becomes the Royal College of Physicians & Surgeons of Glasgow (UK) MFDS Parts 1 & 2 examination centre — the only one in South Karnataka. IAO and HLAT accreditations received. Ranked #1 Private Dental College by The Pioneer.

2015
CBCT Imaging Unit Installed

Advanced Cone Beam CT (CBCT) imaging unit installed in the Oral Medicine & Radiology department — enabling precise 3D diagnosis and treatment planning.

2018
PhD Programs & Real-Time PCR Lab

PhD programs launched in Periodontology, Orthodontics, Oral Surgery, Prosthodontics, and Public Health Dentistry. Real-Time PCR lab established attracting national research collaborations.

2021
NIRF Rank #37 — Dental Category

Achieved NIRF Rank #37 for Dental colleges in India — recognition of consistent academic output, research, and placement performance. PG Hostel newly completed.

2026
Digital Transformation — 32 Years of Excellence

Launch of comprehensive digital campus portal, AI-powered patient care, and admission to 100 BDS seats and 9 MDS specialities. Highest placement package: ₹32.5 LPA.
