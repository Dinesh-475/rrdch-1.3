Rajarajeshwari Dental
College & Hospital

Recognized by the Dental Council of India, Government of Karnataka, and Government of India. Affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS). Our campus represents a continuous commitment to excellence in dental healthcare and education.

Our Legacy

Established in 1992 with just 40 admissions in the BDS course, RRDCH has rapidly grown into a massive educational ecosystem. Today, we stand as a premier institution housing over 250 modern dental units and chairs, treating 450 to 500 patients on a daily basis.

Each of our 12 departments is staffed with exceptionally qualified, experienced faculty members who ensure that our students receive top-tier knowledge and real-world clinical exposure. We hold an A Grade accreditation from NAAC, further certifying our dedication to superior educational standards.

Our Vision

To establish a center of excellence in dental education and healthcare, producing clinicians and researchers capable of serving society with compassion, unparalleled skill, and integrity.

Our Mission

  • ✓ Impart globally standardized, high-quality dental education.
  • ✓ Provide state-of-the-art clinical infrastructure.
  • ✓ Encourage profound academic research and publications.
  • ✓ Promote public health dentistry through community service campaigns.
