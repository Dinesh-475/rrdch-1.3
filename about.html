Recognized by the Dental Council of India, Government of Karnataka, and Government of India. Affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS). Recognized by the Royal College of Surgeons of Glasgow for MFDS Part 1 and 2 Examinations.

Our Legacy

Established in 1992 under the Rajiv Gandhi Group of Institutions and Moogambigai Charitable and Educational Trust, RRDCH began with just 40 BDS admissions. Over three decades, we have grown into a comprehensive educational ecosystem housing over 250 modern dental units, treating 450-500 patients daily through our General Hospital and 12 specialized OPD clinics.

Our 12 postgraduate departments are staffed with exceptionally qualified faculty, ensuring students receive top-tier knowledge and real-world clinical exposure. We hold an A Grade NAAC accreditation and are recognized by the Royal College of Surgeons of Glasgow.

Institutional Genesis

Founded in 1992 under the Rajiv Gandhi Group of Institutions and the Moogambigai Charitable and Educational Trust, RRDCH was established with a vision to provide exceptional dental education and community-focused healthcare. Beginning with only 40 admissions in the Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) program, the college has undergone exponential growth over three decades.

Today, the campus houses over 250 modern dental units and chairs, with a comprehensive General Hospital treating 450-500 patients daily. Our 12 specialized OPD clinics provide postgraduate dental students with extensive clinical training.

Future Strategic Trajectories

As RRDCH moves toward its fourth decade, our strategic goals focus on deeper technological integration, global research collaboration, and internationalization of education.

Digital Transformation

Progressive move toward a digital-first clinical model with expansion of CAD-CAM laboratory facilities and wider application of digital imaging across all specialties. Paperless diagnostics and advanced laser technologies will be institution-wide.

Research & IP

Expanding international scientific footprint through patents and high-impact publications. Transforming every postgraduate department into a fully functional Ph.D. research center, creating a continuous cycle of knowledge generation.

Global Education

Leveraging recognition from the Royal College of Surgeons of Glasgow to host international training workshops and examinations. Attracting diverse student body and providing exposure to global dental standards.
